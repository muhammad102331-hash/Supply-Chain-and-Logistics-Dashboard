# Supply Chain and Logistics Dashboard

This **Supply Chain and Logistics Dashboard** is designed to provide an overview of critical supply chain operations, allowing users to make informed decisions regarding revenue, profit, suppliers, shipments, inventory, and customer data.

## Key Features

- **Total Revenue & Profit Analysis**: Track total revenue and profit across different periods to monitor performance.
- **Supplier Management**: Get insights into supplier performance, including the average lead time and total number of suppliers.
- **Inventory Stock**: Analyze the inventory stock across various product categories and identify areas of overstock or stockouts.
- **Shipment Analysis**: Track total shipments and delivered quantities, and analyze delays by carrier.
- **Customer Insights**: Evaluate customer data including total delivered quantities and revenue contribution.
- **Delay Shipment Analysis**: Identify which carriers cause the most delays, allowing for better logistical planning.
- **Total Revenue by Platform**: Compare revenue from different platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, and Best Buy to identify the most profitable sales channels.

## Insights

- **Revenue Growth**: The dashboard highlights **176.95M in total revenue** and **48.56M in total profit**, reflecting strong overall performance.
- **Supplier Performance**: The **average lead time** is measured, with **BOE Technologies** and **Samsung Electronics** delivering the best lead times, helping optimize procurement planning.
- **Inventory Stock**: Insights show that certain products, like **Galaxy S24** and **Galaxy Buds2 Pro**, are at higher inventory levels, while products like **Galaxy Tab A9** are understocked.
- **Shipment Delays**: **Maersk Line** and **DHL Express** are identified as the carriers causing the most shipment delays, affecting the overall supply chain efficiency.
- **Platform Revenue**: The **Amazon.com** platform generates the highest revenue at **37M**, followed closely by **Flipkart** at **36M**.
- **Customer Performance**: **Samsung Direct Store** and **Best Buy** show strong customer contributions to revenue and should be prioritized for further engagement.

## Tools and Technologies Used

- **Power BI**: For creating interactive and visually engaging dashboards.
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**: For creating custom calculations and measures.
- **Power Query**: For cleaning and transforming data for better insights.
- **Data Modeling**: Optimizing the data model to ensure accurate and efficient reporting.

## Benefits

- **Centralized Insights**: Provides a 360-degree view of the supply chain, helping decision-makers understand key metrics like inventory, revenue, and shipments.
- **Optimized Operations**: By identifying issues such as delayed shipments and overstocked products, businesses can take proactive measures to improve efficiency.
- **Actionable Data**: The insights into revenue by platform, supplier performance, and customer data help businesses make data-driven decisions.
